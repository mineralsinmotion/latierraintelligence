About

Independent Geoscience Research

La Tierra Resource Intelligence is an independent, client-funded geoscience research initiative dedicated to the compilation, synthesis, interpretation, and publication of global technical information relating to Earth and environmental resources. Our work integrates geological observations, historical documentation, spatial information, and modern datasets to produce original research, analytical reports, reference materials, and technical briefs.

Our Approach

Many questions concerning natural resources cannot be understood by examining contemporary information alone. Geological investigations, government surveys, historical mining records, scientific publications, production statistics, and mapping projects often span decades or even centuries. Much of this information remains scattered across numerous sources. La Tierra specializes in bringing these diverse sources together.

By compiling and synthesizing historical information alongside modern scientific literature, geospatial information, and publicly available datasets, we seek to place present-day questions within their broader geological and historical context. The emphasis is not simply on assembling information, but on identifying relationships, long-term trends, changing interpretations, and technical context that may not be evident when individual sources are considered independently.

Principal Researcher

Research is directed by a principal researcher whose professional experience spans geology, mineral-resource analysis, laboratory science, geospatial analysis, environmental management, emergency management, and data science within federal, state, and academic organizations. This interdisciplinary background provides the foundation for integrating diverse information into objective, well-documented technical yet approachable narrative and publications.

Research Philosophy

La Tierra places particular emphasis on the use of primary-source documentation. Historical government publications, geological surveys, scientific literature, archival records, maps, and technical reports are evaluated alongside contemporary data to develop analyses that recognize both historical continuity and evolving scientific understanding. Incorporating these materials into contemporary analyses broadens the evidentiary foundation of the work without treating historical interpretations as inherently authoritative.

Independence

La Tierra Resource Intelligence is independently funded through the sale of publications, research products, and professional services. Our products are intended to improve technical understanding through careful documentation, transparent methodology, and objective analysis. They are not investment research, financial advice, promotional material, or endorsements of companies, projects, or public policy.
